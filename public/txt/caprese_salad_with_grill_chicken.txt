
=== INSTRUCTIONS ===
Arrange the chicken slices, tomatoes, mozzarella, and basil on a plate.
Drizzle with olive oil and balsamic glaze, then sprinkle with salt and pepper.
Serve as a light, refreshing lunch
=== INGREDIENTS ===
​1 grilled chicken breast (sliced)
1 cup cherry tomatoes (halved)
​fresh mozzarella slices
​fresh basil leaves
​balsamic glaze
​olive oil
​salt, and pepper.
