=== INSTRUCTIONS ===
In a bowl or jar, mix chia seeds, milk, and sweetener.
Stir well, then cover and refrigerate for at least 2 hours (or overnight), stirring occasionally to avoid clumping.
In the morning, top with fresh fruits, nuts, or granola.
=== INGREDIENTS ===
1/4 cup chia seeds
1 cup milk (any type)
1-2 tablespoons honey or maple syrup
toppings like fresh fruit, nuts, or granola.
