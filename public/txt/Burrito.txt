=== INSTRUCTIONS ===
Scramble the eggs with the bell peppers, onions, and spinach in a non-stick pan.
Place the tortilla on a plate, sprinkle cheese on it, and add the scrambled egg mixture.
Add salsa and meat, if using, then wrap the tortilla into a burrito.
Optional: Lightly toast it in the pan for extra crispiness. Enjoy!
=== INGREDIENTS ===
One large tortilla
2 eggs
a handful of shredded cheese
diced bell peppers
onions
spinach
salsa
optional cooked sausage or bacon.
