=== INSTRUCTIONS ===
    Toast the Bread:Toast your bread slices to your desired level of crispiness.
    Prepare the Avocado:Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop out the flesh into a bowl.Mash the avocado with a fork until it's smooth or slightly chunky (depending on your preference).Add a pinch of salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon or lime juice to the mashed avocado. Mix well.
    Assemble the Toast:Spread the mashed avocado evenly on the toasted bread slices.Drizzle a little olive oil on top (optional).
    Add Toppings (Optional):Sprinkle with red chili flakes or paprika for a bit of heat.Add any optional toppings like a poached egg, cherry tomatoes, feta cheese, or herbs for extra flavor and texture.
    Serve:Enjoy your avocado toast as is or with your favorite toppings for a delicious and quick meal!
=== INGREDIENTS ===
    1 ripe avocado
    2 slices of bread (whole grain, sourdough, or your favorite)
    1 tablespoon olive oil (optional)
    Salt and pepper (to taste)
    Red chili flakes or paprika (optional)
    Lemon or lime juice (optional, for extra flavor)
    Optional Toppings:
    Poached or fried egg
    Cherry tomatoes (halved)
    Fresh herbs (like cilantro or parsley)
    Feta cheese
    Smoked salmon
    Microgreens or arugula
