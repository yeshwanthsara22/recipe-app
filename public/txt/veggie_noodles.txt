Veggie Stir-Fry with Noodles:
=== INSTRUCTIONS ===
Cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
In a pan, heat a bit of oil and stir-fry all the vegetables until crisp-tender.
Add the noodles, soy sauce, and sesame oil, tossing to coat everything well.
Garnish with green onions and enjoy your flavorful veggie stir-fry!
=== INGREDIENTS ===
1 pack noodles (rice, soba, or spaghetti)
1 bell pepper (sliced)
1 zucchini (sliced)
1 cup broccoli florets
1/2 cup snap peas
2 tablespoons soy sauce
1 teaspoon sesame oil
green onions for garnish.
