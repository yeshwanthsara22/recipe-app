Veggie Stir-Fry with Noodles:
=== INSTRUCTIONS ===
1. Cook the noodles according to the package instructions.
2. In a pan, heat a bit of oil and stir-fry all the vegetables until crisp-tender.
3. Add the noodles, soy sauce, and sesame oil, tossing to coat everything well.
4. Garnish with green onions and enjoy your flavorful veggie stir-fry!
=== INGREDIENTS ===
5. 1 pack noodles (rice, soba, or spaghetti)
6. 1 bell pepper (sliced)
7. 1 zucchini (sliced)
8. 1 cup broccoli florets
9. 1/2 cup snap peas
10. 2 tablespoons soy sauce
11. 1 teaspoon sesame oil
12. green onions for garnish.
